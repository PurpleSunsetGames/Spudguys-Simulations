About The Spudguy

The name "The Spudguy" is, in fact, not at all related to anything on this website. When I started my YouTube channel, I was obsessed with the game Scrap Mechanic, and since the main weapons in that game are spudguns, I figured the name was appropriate. Now, my channel centers around Desmos, computer programming, and music.

The point of this website isn't to talk about my channel, though! It's to act as my portfolio, showing off what I've been doing recently in the world of computer programming. On the home page, you can find a few of the GPU-based web applications that I've worked on. Soon, I will include some of my most technical Desmos graphs (such as this one or the one animated to the right) and explanations on how I made them. I also do 3D animations and renders.

My passion for science, math, and computer programming has led me deep into the field of computational physics, and that passion will keep this portfolio filled with many interesting (and hopefully impressive) projects!
